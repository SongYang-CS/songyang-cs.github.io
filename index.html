Short Biography

    Song Yang is currently an associate professor in the School of Computer Science and Technology in Beijing Institute of Technology. He received his B.E. degree in Software Engineering and his Master of Engineering degree in Computer Application Technology from Dalian University of Technology in 2008 and 2010, respectively. In the year 2015, he received the PhD degree from the Delft University of Technology, The Netherlands. From August 2015 to July 2017, he served as a postdoctoral researcher in the Data Research and Development Computing Center (Gesellschaft für wissenschaftliche Datenverarbeitung mbH Göttingen, GWDG) of the University of Göttingen, Germany.

    Dr. Yang is the author and co-author of over 50 papers in premier network and system journals and conferences. His research papers have been published in many prestigious conferences and journals such as IEEE INFOCOM, ACM Ubicomp, and IEEE TPDS, IEEE Transactions on Mobile Computing, IEEE Transactions on Services Computing.

Research Focus

  • Cloud computing, edge computing: build a small edge cloud system model at the system level, and on this basis, realize various applications such as edge device detection and recognition, task distribution and unloading, optimized transmission of video streams, virtual/augmented reality, artificial intelligence algorithms, and optimize their performance (such as delay, load, etc.) and develop the system framework platform. At the theoretical level, the combination optimization theory, deep reinforcement learning and other algorithms are applied to optimize the scheduling and allocation of resources.
  • Network function virtualization, software-defined network, quantum network and other new network architectures: the realization of resource distribution and application request system and platform for network function virtualization based on software-defined network; Optimize the implementation of routing algorithm, resource automatic control strategy and fault recovery and detection mechanism; the integration of multi-protocol and communication technology in the next generation network framework.
  • Development and application of artificial intelligence algorithms and the Internet of Things: optimization and improvement of algorithms such as deep reinforcement learning and graph neural network; Develop application systems related to the Internet of Things based on artificial intelligence algorithms, such as intelligent transportation.
